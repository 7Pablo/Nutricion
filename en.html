María Gracia Yturralde

I graduated with a degree in Nutrition and Dietetics, and since then I have been dedicated to supporting women in different stages of their lives: from hormonal balance and the menstrual cycle, to conscious and sustainable eating.

I deeply believe in the power of nutrition as a tool for transformation, not only physical, but also emotional. My approach is personalized, without extreme restrictions, based on science, active listening, and education.

I am passionate about helping you reconnect with your well-being, feel at peace with your body, and make decisions that make you feel strong, informed, and happy.
